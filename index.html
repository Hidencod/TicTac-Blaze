TicTac Blaze Privacy Policy

Introduction

Welcome to TicTac Blaze. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy will inform you about how we look after your personal data when you play our game and tell you about your privacy rights.

Data We Collect

We may collect and process the following data:

  • Device information (e.g., device type, operating system)
  • Game play data (e.g., scores, levels completed)
  • Ad interaction data

How We Use Your Data

We use your data to:

  • Improve our game and user experience
  • Provide and manage in-game advertisements
  • Analyze game performance and user behavior

Advertisements

TicTac Blaze currently contains third-party advertisements. These advertisers may use cookies and similar technologies to collect data about your interests to provide you with personalized advertising. We do not control these third parties' tracking technologies or how they may be used.

In-App Purchases

While TicTac Blaze does not currently offer in-app purchases (IAPs), we may introduce this feature in the future. If implemented, any financial data processed for IAPs will be handled securely and in compliance with relevant data protection laws.

Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal data against unauthorized or unlawful processing, accidental loss, destruction, or damage.

Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have certain rights regarding your personal data, including the right to access, correct, or delete your personal information.

Changes to This Policy

We may update our privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new privacy policy on this page.
